About

I build products by finding what others missed in the requirements, the data, or the problem statement.

My background spans design, business, and technology. Studying Innovation Design Management in Germany taught me to balance user needs, business goals, and system constraints without losing sight of any of them.

Since then, I have worked across healthcare, AI consumer products, enterprise platforms, commerce, and education. Different domains, same challenge: understanding what creates value and translating it into products people can use.

Today I am focused on AI product development. That means understanding not just what AI can do, but when it should be used, how it should be designed, and which systems are required to make it useful in practice. I actively work with RAG architectures, agentic workflows, LLM evaluation, and rapid prototyping. Not to collect buzzwords, but to make better product decisions.

What I bring
Clear problem framing, defensible trade-offs, and products that ship without rework.
